Unfolding

I'm Robert, computational designer with IBI Group and dual-degree graduate student at the UBC School of Architecture and Landscape Architecture. This is my website, Unfolding.

I'm a MARCLA student studying architecture and landscape architecture at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver. I also work for IBI Group as a computational designer, where I develop advanced parametric models for equitable, sustainable urban systems. This digital record is an Unfolding of new skills, experiences and ideas.
