unfolding

I'm Robert, a dual-degree graduate student at the UBC School of Architecture and Landscape Architecture. This is my website, unfolding.

I'm a MARCLA student studying architecture and landscape architecture at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver. I also work for IBI Group as a computational designer, where I develop advanced parametric models for equitable, sustainable urban systems. This digital record is an unfolding of new skills, experiences and thoughts. Through open-ended documentation and reflection, I hope to learn something about life, society and the built environment along the way.
